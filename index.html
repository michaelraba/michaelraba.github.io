Energy Defintions

  • Relative azimuthal energy distribution \(\xi^{n} (m)\) defined as
\begin{align} \xi^{(n)}(m) \equiv \frac{\sum_f \lambda^{(n)}(f, m)}{\sum_n \sum_m \sum_f \lambda^{(n)}(f, m)} . \end{align}
